About Amanda Cutter

A Brief Introduction of Me and My Experiences

I am a software engineer. I mostly focus on full-stack web development following the MERN stack. However, I do prefer front-end to back-end, and I do have an ultimate goal of learning something like C# so that I can start programming VR/AR games and apps for education. As a former teacher and a life-long learner, I am an avid supporter of EdTech, and I believe that it should be affordable and accessible to students and teachers around the world so they have the opportunity to reach their full potential.

As a former teach, a writer, and a computer support analyst, I offer a unique perspective on on software and websites from both the user, developer, and go-between. As a teacher and a writer, I learned to communicate and explain things on a level that non-techinical people can understand in both oral and written English. When it comes to EdTech and technology used in the classerooms, I've been there, done that, and probably have a T-shirt for it somewhere. And as a computer support analyst I've listedn to the struggles or users and words with them and developers directly to troubleshoot problems with the programs that they use daily for their jobs. Finally, as a developer, I understand the blood, sweat, tears, time, and energy that goes into creating a program.

If given an opportunity, I have so many things to offer to make any company stronger, even if I'm just a part of the greater system. I strive to help other, make things better, and make a difference in whatever position I may find myself in. I am eager to find myself at a company where I have the opportunity to prove my value, make an impact, and continue to learn to be a better developer. I just need a chance to shine.
